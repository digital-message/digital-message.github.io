Digital Message 數碼訊息

Digital Message LLC, located in New Jersey, is an innovation consulting company focused on learning, research and development in the area of data-centric human computer interaction experience and technology. We collaborate with incubators, startup companies, and educational organizations. Our core technologies include open source development practice, cloud computing, modern backend data API, and frontend design/development.

位於美國新澤西州的數碼訊息有限公司是一家學習，研究和開發創新諮詢公司，重點在於以數據為中心的人機互动體驗和技術。合作者包括孵化器，創新企業以及教育機構等等。其核心技術包括開源軟件開發實踐，雲計算，現代互聯網數據後台以及前端設計/開發。

Sam Liu, Ph.D. 劉桑田博士

Graduated from Peking University (B.S./M.S. Physics) and New York University (M.S/Ph.D. Physics), Dr. Liu had taught at St. John's University, NY and Bloomsburg University, PA, with years of practice in experimental and computational physics as well as college science and technology education. With decades of computer alogrithm and programming experience, Dr. Liu made a career change to mobile web development and worked as senior engineer in several high tech media startup and digital agencies in New York City with strong experience in modern agile collaborative development.

畢業於北京大學(物理學士和碩士)和紐約大學(物理碩士和博士)，曾任教美國紐約聖約翰大學和賓州布隆斯堡大學，劉桑田博士多年從事試驗和計算物理研究以及大學自然科學和技術教育，擁有十多年的計算機算法和編程經驗，然後轉行移動互聯網業，先後就職數家紐約高科技媒體廣告创业公司高级工程师，積累了豐富的現代敏捷合作開發經驗。
