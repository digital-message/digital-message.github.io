Sam Liu, Ph.D. 劉桑田博士

Graduated from Peking University (B.S. and M.S. in Physics) and New York University (M.S. and Ph.D. in Physics), Dr. Liu had taught at St. John's University, NY and Bloomsburg University, PA, with years of practice in experimental and computational physics as well as college science and technology education. With decade of computer alogrithm and programming experience, Dr. Liu made a career change to mobile web development and worked as senior engineer in several high tech media startup and digital agencies in New York City with strong experience in modern agile collaborative development.

畢業於北京大學（物理學士和碩士）和紐約大學（物理碩士和博士），曾任教美國紐約聖約翰大學和賓州布隆斯堡大學，劉博士多年從事實驗和計算物理研究以及大學自然科學和技術教育，擁有十多年的計算機算法和編程經驗，然後改行移動互聯網業，先後就職數家紐約高科技媒體廣告创业公司高级工程师，積累了豐富的現代敏捷合作開發經驗。
