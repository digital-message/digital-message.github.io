Digital Message

Digital Message, LLC is a creative technology innovation lab in mobile-oriented data-centric human-computer experience, data-driven frontend/backend design and distributed collaborative development. We help incubators, startup companies, and educational organizations brainstorm/prototype innovative ideas and bootstrap teams.

Sam Liu, Ph.D.

With decades of practice in computational physics (Peking University and New York University) and college teaching in science and technology (St. John's University and Bloomsburg University), Dr. Liu made a career transition to mobile/web development in advertising technology and digital agencies with strong hands-on experience in agile mobile development.
