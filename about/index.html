Digital Message

Digital Message, LLC is a creative technology innovation lab focued on mobile-oriented data-centric human-computer experience with distributed collaborative development and data-driven frontend/backend design. We help incubators, startup companies, and educational organizations brainstorm/prototype innovative ideas and bootstrap teams.

Sam Liu, Ph.D.

With decades of practice in experimental and computational physics (Peking University and New York University) as well as colllege teaching in science and technology education (St. John's University and Bloomsburg University), Dr. Liu made a career transition to mobile/web development in advertising technology and digital agencies with strong hands-on experience in agile mobile development.
